Description

Falling Data(P5.JS Link)

I recorded down the data of how many times I drink water and go to the bathroom this week. The ball inital drops representing the times I drink water and in the midway it will stop representing how much water is reatined in my body. When it turns into other color and complete the second half way of dropping, it means that I go to the bathroom and get rid of the water in my body.
