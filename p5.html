Project #5 Optical Illusion

Description

Flags(P5.JS Link)

I am a product of the mix culture of America and Taiwan and what you are seeing right now is the proces of how that happened.

Design Process

I thought about this idea of the pattern that represent my culture a lot this week. I feel like its a lot to cover, so at the end, I just chose flags of the two places that influence my life the most. I consider myself being a product/person produced by the Taiwanese and American cultures. Its hard to see which one is more dominating than the other one, so I decied to use the niose element to give movement to the flag pieces and show the process of them blending together.

Reflection

I was actually able to change around 200 lines of code into only 40lines with for loop which I found so efficent and I do think I understand a lot of fundamental ideas of logic in code this time.
